# Frequently Asked Questions

## Shipping

### How long does shipping take?
Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days. Express shipping takes 2-3 business days. Overnight shipping is available for orders placed before 2 PM EST and delivers the next business day.

### How much does shipping cost?
Standard shipping is free for orders over $50. For orders under $50, standard shipping costs $5.99. Express shipping costs $12.99, and overnight shipping costs $24.99.

### Do you ship internationally?
Yes, we ship to over 50 countries worldwide. International shipping typically takes 10-15 business days. International shipping rates start at $19.99 and vary by destination and package weight.

### How can I track my order?
Once your order ships, you will receive a tracking number via email. You can also track your order by logging into your account and visiting the "My Orders" section. Tracking updates may take 24-48 hours to appear after shipment.

## Orders

### How do I place an order?
Browse our catalog, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You can pay using credit card, debit card, PayPal, or Apple Pay. You will receive an order confirmation email within minutes.

### Can I modify my order after placing it?
Orders can be modified within 1 hour of placement. After that, the order enters processing and cannot be changed. Contact our support team immediately if you need to make changes.

### Can I cancel my order?
Orders can be cancelled within 2 hours of placement if they haven't entered the shipping process. To cancel, go to "My Orders" and click "Cancel Order", or contact our support team.

### What payment methods do you accept?
We accept Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover, PayPal, Apple Pay, and Google Pay. All transactions are securely processed with 256-bit SSL encryption.

## Account

### How do I create an account?
Click "Sign Up" on our homepage, enter your email address and create a password. You can also sign up using your Google or Facebook account for faster registration.

### I forgot my password. How do I reset it?
Click "Forgot Password" on the login page, enter your email address, and we'll send you a password reset link. The link expires after 24 hours.

### How do I update my shipping address?
Log into your account, go to "Account Settings" > "Addresses", and update your shipping address. Note that changing your address won't affect orders that have already been placed.

## Products

### Do you offer a warranty?
Yes, all products come with a 1-year manufacturer warranty covering defects in materials and workmanship. Extended warranty plans (2-year and 3-year) are available for purchase at checkout.

### What if I receive a damaged product?
If you receive a damaged product, contact us within 48 hours with photos of the damage. We will arrange a free replacement or full refund. Do not discard the damaged item until the claim is resolved.

### Are your products authentic?
Yes, we are an authorized retailer for all brands we carry. Every product is 100% authentic and sourced directly from manufacturers or authorized distributors.
