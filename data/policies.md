# Company Policies

## Refund Policy

### Eligibility
Customers are eligible for a refund under the following conditions:
- The item was received damaged or defective.
- The item does not match the product description on our website.
- The order was not delivered within 14 business days of the estimated delivery date.
- The customer requests a refund within 30 days of delivery.

### Refund Process
1. Submit a refund request through "My Orders" or contact customer support.
2. Provide your order number and reason for the refund.
3. For damaged items, include photos of the damage.
4. Our team will review your request within 2-3 business days.
5. Approved refunds are processed within 5-7 business days to the original payment method.

### Non-Refundable Items
- Gift cards and digital downloads.
- Items marked as "Final Sale" or "Clearance".
- Personalized or custom-made products.
- Items returned after 30 days of delivery.

### Partial Refunds
Partial refunds may be issued if:
- The item shows signs of use or wear.
- The item is returned without original packaging.
- Only part of an order is being returned.

## Return Policy

### Return Window
Items can be returned within 30 days of delivery. Items must be in original condition with all tags attached and in original packaging.

### How to Return
1. Log into your account and go to "My Orders".
2. Select the order and click "Return Item".
3. Print the prepaid return shipping label.
4. Pack the item securely in its original packaging.
5. Drop off the package at any authorized shipping location.

### Return Shipping
- Defective or incorrect items: Free return shipping provided.
- Change of mind returns: Customer pays $7.99 return shipping fee, deducted from refund.
- International returns: Customer is responsible for return shipping costs.

## Shipping Policy

### Processing Time
Orders are processed within 1-2 business days after placement. Orders placed on weekends or holidays are processed on the next business day.

### Delivery Estimates
- Standard Shipping: 5-7 business days after processing.
- Express Shipping: 2-3 business days after processing.
- Overnight Shipping: Next business day (orders placed before 2 PM EST).
- International Shipping: 10-15 business days after processing.

### Lost or Delayed Packages
If your package has not arrived within 7 business days past the estimated delivery date:
1. Check your tracking information for updates.
2. Contact our support team with your order number.
3. We will initiate an investigation with the carrier.
4. If the package is confirmed lost, we will send a replacement or issue a full refund at no additional cost.

### Address Errors
If an incorrect shipping address is provided by the customer, we are not responsible for lost or misdirected packages. If the package is returned to us, we will reship it at the customer's expense.

## Privacy Policy

### Data Collection
We collect only the information necessary to process orders and improve our services, including: name, email, shipping address, payment details, and browsing behavior on our site.

### Data Usage
Your data is used to:
- Process and fulfill orders.
- Send order confirmation and shipping updates.
- Improve our products and services.
- Send promotional offers (only with your consent).

### Data Protection
All personal data is encrypted using industry-standard 256-bit SSL encryption. We never sell or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.
